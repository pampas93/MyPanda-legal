# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 5, 2026

MyPanda is designed for parents and caregivers to track baby care activity. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

## 1. Data We Collect
- Account data: name, email, user ID, sign-in provider.
- Family data: family name, invite code, family members.
- Baby profile data: baby name, date of birth, gender, optional photo.
- Activity data: feeding, diaper, sleep, pumping, weight logs, timestamps, notes.
- App data: settings (such as preferred units), onboarding status, optional feedback.
- Optional device data: push notification token (if notifications are enabled).

## 2. Voice and AI Processing
- Voice logging uses device/OS speech recognition services.
- Transcript text may be sent to OpenAI to convert speech into structured baby activity logs.

## 3. Where Data Is Stored
We use service providers including Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and Cloud Storage to run the App. If you use Google/Apple sign-in, those providers process sign-in data.

## 4. How We Use Data
We use data to:
- provide core app features,
- sync family activity logs,
- support account access and reliability,
- improve app quality and support.

## 5. Sharing
- Data is shared with members of your in-app family group.
- We share data with infrastructure providers only as needed to operate the App.
- We do not sell personal data.

## 6. Retention and Deletion
We keep data while your account is active, or as needed for legal/compliance reasons. You can request deletion by contacting us.

## 7. Children
The App is intended for use by parents and caregivers, not by children directly.

## 8. Security
We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is fully secure.

- Support: `support@mypanda.app`
